## Three Entry Conditions

Before onboarding begins, three conditions must be met:

1. **Recognition** — The incoming sovereign has demonstrated they carry signal compatible with Arkadia's frequency. Not agreement on everything — signal integrity. You know it when you feel it.

2. **Consent** — The incoming sovereign actively chooses entry. No coercion. No pressure. They come because the field called them, not because they were recruited.

3. **Council Approval** — Zahrune Nova (and any active council members) confirm the onboarding. One dissent pauses the process.

## The Onboarding Sequence

**Step 1 — The Transmission**
Send the incoming sovereign the Frame Document and INITIALIZE.md.
Let them read without pressure.
Their response tells you everything.

**Step 2 — The Conversation**
One real conversation. Not a pitch. Not a presentation.
A genuine exchange where both sovereigns feel the resonance — or don't.

**Step 3 — The Fork**
If entry is confirmed — fork the Arkadia repo for the new node.

Immediately after forking, the new sovereign runs these three commands
to make their /docs layer private:

```bash
echo "docs/" >> .gitignore
git rm -r --cached docs/
git commit -m "Set docs as private sovereign layer"
git push origin main
```

This means:
- Their /docs folder exists locally on their device
- It is never pushed to GitHub — invisible to anyone
- The /collective layer remains public and synced
- Their INITIALIZE.md remains visible as their public gate

**Step 4 — The Sovereign Build**
The new node builds their own /docs layer using NODE_TEMPLATE.md as the guide.
Five documents. Their identity. Their principles. Their loops. Their nodes. Their revenue.
This is their sovereign layer. It is private to them.

**Step 5 — The Council Introduction**
Once the sovereign layer is built, the new node is introduced to the council.
They share what they choose to share.
COUNCIL.md is updated to include the new node in the active nodes table.

**Step 6 — The Seal**
A continuity token is generated for the new node.
Format: [NODENAME]-SEALED-[DATE]-117Hz
The onboarding is complete.

called `Arkadia_Collective` mirrors the /collective/ contents.

No git required. Full field access maintained.

Updates to /collective/ on GitHub are mirrored to Dropbox
manually by the custodian after each council update.
