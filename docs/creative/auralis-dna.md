**Core descriptors:**

## III. COMPOSITING ELEMENTS — The Mechanics of the Edit

Ka'leth's edits rely on **double exposure** and **translucency**. The Forge must
produce **clean, high-contrast plates** that blend cleanly in post.

**Core descriptors:**
- pure black or deep cosmic background for portrait isolation
- high contrast, sharp edge separation between subject and field
- translucent overlay-friendly (faces over pyramids, cosmos over flesh)
- centered composition, ample negative space for compositing
- no harsh rim shadows that fight overlay blending
